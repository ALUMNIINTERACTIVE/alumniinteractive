Architecture, Economics, and Developer Guide

Tokenomics (2026 Blueprint)

The ALUMNI network employs an "Ultra Sound" deflationary economic model designed to strictly limit supply and heavily reward early validators.

1. Hard Cap (Absolute Scarcity)

The ALUMNI blockchain has an immutable max supply of 100,000,000 ALUMNI. Once 100M coins are minted, the code physically prevents any further issuance. Validators will then survive purely on gas fees.

2. Genesis Treasury

To fund ecosystem growth and DEX liquidity, exactly 10% (10,000,000 ALUMNI) was pre-mined in the Genesis block and permanently assigned to the @ALUMNI.SATOSHI Treasury Wallet.

3. Four-Year Halving Schedule

Block rewards started at 50 ALUMNI per block. Every 25,228,800 blocks (roughly 4 years), the block reward is mathematically cut in half.

4. Deflationary Gas Burn

Every transaction on the network costs a flat Gas Fee of 0.01 ALUMNI. 50% of this fee is tipped to the Validator who mines the block. The other 50% is permanently burned from the total supply.

How to Use the Network

A quick-start guide to the ALUMNI Web3 Ecosystem.

  • Create an Identity: Go to the Web Wallet tab and click "Generate New Web3 Wallet". Set your custom @ALUMNI.TAG so friends can easily send you funds.
  • Receive Funds: Click your alias in the wallet to silently copy your raw Public Key, or click "Share Address" to send your QR code or Tag to someone else. Note: Cryptographic keys use strict PEM formatting. Always keep the -----BEGIN... and -----END... headers intact!
  • Send Transactions: You can paste an Alias, scan a friend's QR code, or paste their full PEM string. The system will automatically resolve the identity and process the transfer.
  • Mint Custom Tokens: Navigate to the DEX Swap tab. Input a Token Ticker (e.g., "USDT") and an amount to deploy your very own ALC-20 smart contract instantly.
  • Become a Validator: The ALUMNI network runs on Proof-of-Stake. Navigate to your Web Wallet, enter an amount, and stake at least 50 ALUMNI to turn your device into an active network validator!
